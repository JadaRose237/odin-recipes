Lasagna

Description

An italian classic: Lasagna is something that everyone can enjoy. A simple recipie that uses minimal ingredients and yet has a huge flavour profile to delight all.

Making lasagna is super simple, anyone can do it in a few easy and simple steps.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Lasagna tomato sauce
  • Lasagna white sauce
  • 500g mince meat
  • Chedder cheese 200g
  • Mozarella cheese 100g

Steps

  1. First start by cooking the mince in a pan. Season to taste.
  2. When cooked add all the red sauce and lower the heat to a gentle simmer
  3. Meanwhile boil the kettle of water enough for a wide pan. When boiled add water and oil then add a few sheets of pasta making sure they dont stick.
  4. When Al dente remove and set aside and start grating cheeses
  5. Then you can do your pasta layers accordingly then set over to 180 fan degrees for 40min and enjoy!
