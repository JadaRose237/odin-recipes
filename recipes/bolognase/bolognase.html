Bolognase

Description

A saucy pasta dish with a rich taste that imerses you within italian cusine and culture.

Bolognase is a really popular dish that is eaten a lot within european countries as originated from one

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Bolognase sauce
  • Chedder cheese 100g or Parmasan cheese 50g
  • 500g mince beef

steps

  1. Boil kettle then add to large pot with pasta and oil and season with salt
  2. Whilst thats boiling we cook the mince and season with pepper and salt and garlic and herbs and once cooked add the sauce
  3. When done drain the pasta and turn of heat for mince and start grating all cheeses
  4. Stir in cheese and mince into pasta and plate up and garnish lightly with mixed herbs and enjoy!
